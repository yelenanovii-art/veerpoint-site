Questions teams ask

The honest version, before the call.

Q.01

Why a 6-month minimum?

90 days to validate the motion, list, messaging, conversion math. Another 90 to close enough deals to call it repeatable. Anything shorter is a trial.

Q.02

What does it cost?

Monthly retainer plus commission on closed business. Both fixed in writing after Discovery. Retainer scales with motion complexity. Commission rate set in the proposal.

Q.03

What do we keep if we part ways?

All accounts, contacts, deals and CRM records stay with you. The playbook and proprietary sequences are ours, available to buy out on exit.

Q.04

Can we hire your operators after the engagement?

Yes. Open conversation, not a hidden fee. We'll talk about it in the Stage 3 debrief.

Q.05

How is this different from an agency or an SDR shop?

Agencies hand you a list. SDR shops hand you a junior. We do the calls, founder-level, start to finish. No pyramid, no bait-and-switch.

Q.06

What if we want to off-ramp mid-cycle?

30-day notice between stages. Clean handover. No early-termination penalty.

Q.07

Where do you work, and which time zones?

Barcelona-based, EU and US time zones. Remote-first. Staffed to the buyer's calendar, not ours.
